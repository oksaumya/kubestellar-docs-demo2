IFoS

Intern for Open Source

Description

IFoS (Intern for Open Source) is KubeStellar's own flagship internship program designed to nurture talent and foster innovation in the cloud-native and multi-cluster Kubernetes ecosystem.

This exclusive 3-month unpaid internship program provides hands-on experience working directly with KubeStellar's core development team on cutting-edge multi-cluster orchestration technologies.

Successful completion of IFoS gives participants a significant advantage and preference when applying for paid internship opportunities through prestigious programs like GSoC, LFX Mentorship, and ESoC, creating a direct pathway to advanced open-source contributions.
